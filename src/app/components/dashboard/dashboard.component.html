Terms & Conditions (Short)

By using this system, teachers agree to use the platform responsibly. Leave requests are subject to approval and must be submitted at least 48 hours in advance. Any misuse of the system may result in disciplinary actions.

How to Apply for Leave

  1. Log in to your teacher dashboard.
  2. Go to the "Leave" section.
  3. Select your leave type and dates.
  4. Add a reason (optional).
  5. Click "Submit" and wait for admin approval.
